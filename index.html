Aedifico is Construction Elite

At Aedifico Construction, we’re more than just a construction company; we’re your dedicated partner in bringing your construction dreams to life. With a proud family-run heritage spanning over 5 years, our unwavering commitment to our clients remains steadfast. Our core values revolve around putting the customer first, delivering exceptional quality work, and ensuring the timely fulfillment of our commitments.


C.O.R.E Values

  • Customer Advocates - We’re laser focused on exceeding customer expectations.
  • Opportunity Makers - We believe in finding and creating the chance for everyone to succeed.
  • Reputation Builders - We strive to be known for our professionalism and trusted as industry leaders.
  • Excellence Seekers - We actively seek out ways to grow and reach our full potential.

Our mission is clear: to build stunning homes that your family can cherish for generations. We want your home to be a source of joy and comfort in every season. We thrive on challenging construction projects. It's through overcoming these challenges that we continually improve, foster stronger relationships, and reinforce our commitment to delivering excellence in every project.


Our Expertise

Aedifico Construction offers a comprehensive range of construction management services, from pre-construction planning to post-construction commissioning and owner occupancy support. We specialize in working directly with owners, offering construction management, design-build, and lump sum contracts. We are renowned experts in a wide array of construction services, including:

  • New Home Construction: Whether you're envisioning your dream home or a residential project, our experienced team will bring your vision to life with precision and care.
  • Home Additions and Major Renovations: Need to add another room to your home? Our team of experts can assist you throughout the entire process, from initial planning to final completion.
  • Exterior Enveloping: Your home's exterior is more than just its four walls. It's the foundation, framing, and personalized details like siding and shingles that create a lasting impression. At Aedifico Construction Elite, we specialize in a wide range of exterior renovations, from siding and roofing to other specialized services.

At Aedifico Construction, we're committed to providing exceptional customer service.

With over 5 years of experience in the construction industry, we offer:

  • One-on-One Consulting: Throughout your project, we provide personalized support to keep you informed and involved every step of the way.
  • Peace of Mind: When you work with Aedifico Construction, you can trust our experienced team to deliver your project from start to finish.
