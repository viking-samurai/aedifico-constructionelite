Aedifico is Construction Elite

At Aedifico Construction, we are more than just a construction company; we are your dedicated partner in bringing your construction dreams to life. With a proud family-run heritage spanning over 5 years, our unwavering commitment to our clients remains steadfast. Our core values revolve around putting the customer first, delivering unparalleled quality work, and ensuring the timely fulfillment of our commitments.


Our mission is clear: to build beautiful homes that your family can cherish for generations. We want your home to be a source of joy and comfort in every season. We thrive on challenging construction projects. It's in overcoming these challenges that we continually improve, foster stronger relationships, and ensure our commitment to getting the job done right, every time.



  • Our Expertise

    Aedifico Construction offers a comprehensive range of construction management services, from pre-construction planning to post-construction commissioning and owner occupancy support. We specialize in working directly with owners, offering construction management, design-build, and lump sum contracts.

    We are renowned experts in a wide array of construction services, including:

    • New Home Construction: Whether you're envisioning your dream home or a residential project, our seasoned team brings your vision to life with precision and care.
    • Home Additions and Major Renovations: When you literally need another room added on to your house or home, our team of experts can help from the planning pahse, right through to completion.
    • Exterior Enveloping: The exterior of your home is more than just it's four walls. It's the foundation, framing, and all of the customization that goes into picking the perfect type siding or shingles. At Aedifico Construction Elite, we specialize in a wide range of work around the sides of your home.

  • Why choose Aedifico Construction?

    At Aedifico Construction, we pride ourselves on delivering the best customer experience possible.

    With over 5 years of industry expertise, we offer:

    • One-on-One Consulting: Throughout your project, we provide personalized support, so you always feel heard and informed.
    • Peace of Mind: Knowing you're working with the best in the business, you can trust us to realize your project needs from inception to completion.
