  • About Aedifico Construction Elite

  • Expanding your property is a significant investment, and we make it a rewarding experience

    Aedifico Construction Elite offers comprehensive building construction packages for residential, commercial, and industrial projects, including expert construction management. With a long history of serving the Ottawa region, we've perfected our approach to deliver the highest quality management teams and construction standards. Our packages provide the following benefits:

    • Streamlined Project Management: Centralized Platform
      Our centralized project management platform empowers seamless communication and document control, ensuring your project stays on track and transparent.
    • Tailored Construction Solutions: Your Vision, Our Expertise
      We create a personalized construction plan tailored to your specific project needs, ensuring optimal results and exceeding your expectations.
    • Precision Construction: Minimal Disruption
      Our experienced team executes projects with precision, ensuring minimal disruption to your daily life. We maintain a clean and safe work environment throughout the construction process.
    • Dedicated Project Management: Your Single Point of Contact
      Benefit from the expertise of our dedicated project management team, including a Project Manager, Project Coordinator, and Site Supervisor. Your Site Supervisor will be on-site daily throughout the project, ensuring seamless communication and oversight.
    • Leveraging Our Network: Skilled Tradespeople and Reliable Suppliers
      Access to our extensive network of skilled tradespeople, construction professionals, and reliable suppliers ensures timely project completion and exceptional quality.

About Jake Nelson

Jake, getting right it

Jake, measuring twice

Jake Nelson, President of Aedifico, is a seasoned construction industry veteran with over 18 years of experience. Through his extensive career, he has witnessed firsthand the best and worst practices in construction project management, both residential and commercial. Recognizing recurring challenges, Jake developed his own unique approach to residential multi-family and commercial/industrial building construction.


After three years of development and testing on personal real estate projects, Aedifico Construction Elite was created. This proprietary building methodology and its processes were meticulously crafted into a comprehensive, end-to-end package.


Jake's Vision

Aedifico Construction Elite is committed to being the leading partner for commercial and residential developers seeking cutting-edge building solutions. We're driving the industry forward by pioneering innovative and transformative construction practices.


At Aedifico Construction Elite, we thrive on collaborating with dedicated individuals who share our commitment to excellence. We view our clients as partners, working together to achieve the best possible outcomes in the most efficient manner. By fostering a collaborative partnership, we ensure long-lasting relationships built on mutual success.
